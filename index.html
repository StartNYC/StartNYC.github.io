Here's What We Offer

Travel

We'll take you to NYC free of charge. It's a 5 hour drive and we'll guarentee that you'll bond with the other fellows on the way there!

Lodging

We'll put you up in a really nice Airbnb in the city and have some night-time activities planned!

Food

Unfortunately we probably will not be covering the cost of food but we'll take you to some of the best and most well-priced restaurants in the city!

Career Jumpstart

We'll help place you at a startup for the summer and we'll throw events back-to-back throughout the day to get you fully emmersed in the world of the NYC Tech Scene!
