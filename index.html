Here's What We Offer

Expenses

We'll take you to NYC free of charge. It's a 5 hour drive, which is plenty of time to bond with other Fellows on the way there! We'll also put you up in a nice Airbnb in the city and have some night activities planned!

Community

We will introduce you to an extraordinary group of individuals from Penn State who are as passionate about learning and entrepreneurship as you are.

Career Jumpstart

We're here to help you! We'll hold events back-to-back throughout the day to get you fully emmersed in the world of the NYC Tech Scene and expose you to some terrific networking opportunities!
